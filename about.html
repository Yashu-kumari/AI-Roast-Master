About AI Roast Master

What is AI Roast Master?

AI Roast Master is an innovative comedy app that uses artificial intelligence to analyze your photos and generate personalized roasts, witty comebacks, and stand-up routines. Our AI comedian never sleeps and is always ready to roast!

How It Works

1. Upload your photo

2. Choose your humor style (Savage, Playful, Sarcastic, or Absurd)

3. Get roasted by our AI comedian

4. Generate comebacks and comedy routines
